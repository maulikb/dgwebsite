welcome to dg knitting pvt. ltd.

DG Knitting is amongst the fastest growing vertically integrated textile companies in India.With a most modern Circular Knitting plant with machine from the most reputed machine manufacturers like TERROT,PAILUNG,FUKUHAMA etc we offer a very comprehensive and high fashion range knitted fabrics in 100% cotton, polyester, viscose, Lycra, lurex and their blends. From single jersey, interlocks & ribs ,Industrial Fabric i.e backing and compression Fabric,rich fabrics for performance wear with spandex attachment used in sportswear,ladies innerwear,nightwear etc. are some products which we specialize in.


In more than two decades, DG Knitting has grown to become a diversified manufacturer of world-class polyester yarns, knitted & suiting fabrics selling directly to manufacturers, exporters, importers, retailers and brands the world over.
