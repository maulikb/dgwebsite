About Us

dg knitting pvt. ltd.

  • Established in 1986 as a private limited company, DG Knitting began with Weaving and steadily expanded into Knitting & Twisting.
  • In more than two decades, DG Knitting has grown to become a diversified manufacturer of world-class polyester yarns, knitted & suiting fabrics.
  • Weaving Unit with High speed rapier Looms is situated at Ichalkaranji and Knitting Unit is situated at Silvassa.

dg polyester pvt. ltd.

  • Established in 1996 as a private limited company, DG began with texturising of yarn and steadily expanded into Knitting. Since then we have continued to increase the scale of our operations and the range of our activities.
  • DG polyester has grown to become a diversified manufacturer of world-class polyester yarns selling directly to manufacturers, exporters, importers, retailers and brands across India.
